## Payment & Billing

<div class="faq-list">
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>What payment methods do you accept?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through our payment provider.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>Can I get a refund?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team within 14 days of your purchase for a full refund. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>How do I cancel my subscription?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. You won't be charged for the next period.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>Do you offer discounts for annual plans?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">Yes! Annual plans receive a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. Enterprise customers can also negotiate custom pricing based on volume and contract length.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
</div>

## Account & Access

<div class="faq-list">
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>How do I reset my password?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within minutes. The link expires after 24 hours for security reasons.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>Can I change my email address?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">Yes, you can update your email in Account Settings. After entering your new email, you'll need to verify it through a confirmation link sent to the new address.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>How do I enable two-factor authentication?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">Go to Account Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. You can use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy) or receive codes via SMS. We recommend using an authenticator app for better security.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
</div>

## Technical Issues

<div class="faq-list">
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>Why am I getting blocked by websites?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">Websites may block automated requests if they detect unusual activity. Solutions include: 1) Using our Web Unlocker feature for automatic bypass, 2) Reducing request frequency, 3) Using proxy rotation, 4) Adding random delays between requests.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>What should I do if my Worker returns empty results?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">This could be due to: 1) Website structure changes - check if selectors need updating, 2) Anti-bot protection - enable Web Unlocker, 3) Rate limiting - reduce request speed, 4) Login requirements - ensure your credentials are valid.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>How do I handle CAPTCHAs?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">CoreClaw's Web Unlocker automatically handles most CAPTCHAs. For more complex cases, you can integrate third-party CAPTCHA solving services through our API. Check our [bypass verification guide](/developer-guide/web-unlocker/bypass-verification-code/) for details.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
	<div class="faq-item">
		<details>
			<summary>
				<span>What are the API rate limits?</span>
			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">Rate limits depend on your plan: Free tier allows 100 requests/day, Pro allows 10,000 requests/day, Enterprise has custom limits. Check your usage dashboard for real-time statistics.</div>
		</details>
	</div>
</div>

## Getting Started

<div class="faq-list">
	<div class="faq-item">

			</summary>
			<div class="faq-answer">Follow our [Quick Start Guide](/getting-started/quick-start/) for step-by-step instructions. You can use our no-code template library or write custom scripts using our API. Most users are up and running within 10 minutes.</div>
