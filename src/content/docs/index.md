CafeScraper is dedicated to providing efficient, stable, and scalable data acquisition solutions for users with diverse backgrounds and technical capabilities. Our users primarily come from the following groups:

## Enterprise and Business Users

#### Typical Roles:

- Market Analysis Teams
- E-commerce Operations Teams
- Advertising and Brand Departments
- Data Procurement Managers
- Startup Founders

**What are they concerned about?**

- Data acquisition without a technical team
- Clear data structures and delivery standards
- Controllable costs
- Compliance and security assurance
- Long-term stable data supply

**What we provide for them:**

- No-Code Template Scraping
- Custom Data Requirement Posting (Free Data Bounty)
- Full-process Managed Execution
- Data Cleaning and Structured Output
- Enterprise-level Security and Privacy Assurance

---

## Data-driven Entrepreneurs and Individual Users

**Typical Roles:**

- Independent Website Sellers
- Amazon / TikTok Shop Operators
- SEO Practitioners
- Digital Marketing Consultants
- Individual Investment Analysts

**What are they concerned about?**

- Quick Start
- Ease of Use
- Low Barrier to Entry Cost
- Clear ROI

**What we provide for them:**

- 200+ Ready-to-use Templates
- Concise Execution Interface
- Pay-as-you-go Mechanism
- Direct Download of Structured Data

---

## Our Core Philosophy

Whether you are:

- A business with clear data needs but no technical expertise
- An engineer looking to monetize technical skills
- Or a data user pursuing efficiency

CafeScraper provides you with clear pathways and matching resources.
