## CoreClaw Invitation Program Terms & Conditions

Welcome to the event hosted by CoreClaw ("Platform"). To protect your rights, please read these rules carefully before participation. By joining the event, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to all terms and conditions.

## 1. Event Period & Eligibility

The event is available on an irregular basis. Eligible users are those invited by the Platform. The scope of eligible users is subject to change based on platform notices or in-app prompts.

## 2. Event Details

Users who invite new users to register via their unique referral link will receive a $3 balance reward. The balance can be used directly for platform tools and services.

## 3. Rules

### Reward Mechanism

- You will receive $3 balance for each new user who successfully registers via your unique referral link.
- Rewards are cumulative with no limit on the number of invites — the more you invite, the more you earn.
- Rewards are credited directly to your CoreClaw account and can be used for platform services. Balance is valid for 30 days from issuance.
- Rewards are processed and issued automatically upon successful new user registration; no manual claim is required.

### Eligibility Rules

- All registered CoreClaw users may participate.
- Referees must be new, previously unregistered users. The same phone number or device counts as one user only.
- Self-invites, fake accounts, bulk registration, or other fraudulent behavior is prohibited. Violations will result in reward revocation and account suspension.

### Event Duration

This is a limited-time event. CoreClaw reserves the right to modify terms or terminate the event at any time.

### Balance Terms

- "Balance" refers to event rewards usable only for CoreClaw services (e.g., crawler tools). Usage rates vary by service and are subject to product pages.
- Balance history can be viewed in: Console → Wallet → Deposit & Usage Records.
- Event balance is a reward balance, separate from subscription balance from purchased plans.

## 4. Special Terms

### Privacy Protection

The Platform safeguards user information with security policies, technologies, and procedures. All recipients of user data are required to protect confidentiality. For details, see the [CoreClaw Privacy Policy](/platform-policies/privacy-policy/).

### Single User Definition

Accounts sharing the same login, email, device, IP, or related identifiers will be treated as one single user.

### Anti-Fraud

Fraudulent or abusive behavior will result in penalties including: login restrictions, account suspension, reward forfeiture, balance revocation, and clawback of issued rewards. The Platform shall not be liable for any losses caused by such enforcement.
