If you build web scrapers, automation scripts, or data collection tools, you can publish them to the CoreClaw Store and reach users without finding clients, negotiating projects, or handling support yourself.

## Revenue Sharing

Published Workers may participate in platform revenue sharing based on real usage. As long as a Worker keeps being used, it can keep earning — turning a one-time build into recurring value.

Your earning potential generally improves with Worker stability, data accuracy, success rate, usage frequency, and market demand.

## Earnings & Payout

Usage is recorded automatically, and your earnings and payout details are available in the [CoreClaw Console](https://console.coreclaw.com). For current revenue-share rates, payout methods, and thresholds, refer to the Console or contact [support@coreclaw.com](mailto:support@coreclaw.com).

## Getting Started

1. Develop and submit your Worker.
2. Pass platform review.
3. Once listed, your Worker is available to Store users and can participate in revenue sharing.

See [Publish Your Worker](/developer-guide/publishing-and-monetization/publish-your-worker/) for the submission and review process.
