If you know how to build web scrapers, automation scripts, or data collection tools, you can turn your skills directly into income.

At **CoreClaw**, you don't need to hunt for clients, negotiate projects, or handle customer support — simply submit high-quality scripts. Once approved, you can participate in platform revenue sharing.

---

## Revenue Model

### Recurring Revenue Share (10%)

After your script goes live:

- Every time a user runs your script
- Real usage fees are generated
- The platform shares a fixed percentage of those fees with you

Revenue share is a fixed **10%**.

As long as your script continues to be used, you continue to earn. This is not a one-time bounty — it's long-term recurring cash flow.

### Earnings Example

Let's assume your script:

- Generates **$10,000** in platform usage fees per month
- Has a **10% revenue share**

Your monthly income = **$1,000**

If the script runs consistently for 12 months:

Annual income could reach **$12,000+**

This is how you turn code into a long-term asset.
