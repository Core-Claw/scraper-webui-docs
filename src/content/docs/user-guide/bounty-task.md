### What is a Bounty Task?

The Developer Bounty Program is a pay-on-demand development task mechanism:

- Requesters post requirements and set bounties
- Developers accept and complete the development
- The platform handles review and settlement throughout the process

### Who is it for?

#### For Requesters

- Those who need fast, clearly defined development deliverables

#### For Developers

- Those who want to earn stable income through real tasks

### How it Works

#### ① Post a Requirement

The requester fills in: requirement description, bounty amount, and required delivery time.

#### ② Platform Review

The platform reviews the requirement, helps refine it, and publishes it after confirming compliance.

#### ③ Developer Acceptance

Developers select and claim tasks from the Task Center.

#### ④ Development & Submission

Developers complete the script development and submit it to the platform.

#### ⑤ Platform Review

The platform conducts a preliminary review of the script, confirming it meets the requirements and is functional.

#### ⑥ Acceptance & Settlement

The platform verifies the deliverables and processes settlement.
