## 1. What Is the Promoter Program?

The CafeScraper Promoter Program is a referral reward system.

You can invite friends to register and use CafeScraper through your exclusive referral link or invitation code. When your referred users complete registration and generate valid spending, you will receive a commission based on a defined percentage.

Whether you are a developer, data professional, content creator, or manage relevant communities, you can earn ongoing income through referrals.

---

## 2. How Are Commissions Calculated?

##### 1️⃣ Commission Rate

- Currently, you can enjoy a commission reward of up to 30% (subject to the platform's actual promotional policies).
- Calculated based on the actual valid spending amount of the invited user; withdrawal available after 30 days.
- Once your invited friend makes a recharge, the cashback will be automatically credited to your cashback account, amounting to 10% of the total recharge amount.
- When your invited friend runs a script, the amount will be calculated and credited in real-time at a rate of 20%.

**Example:**

If your referred user spends **$1,000** on the platform and your commission rate is 33%,

you will earn **$300** in commission.

---

##### 2️⃣ Commission Rules

- ✅ Not limited to the first order — as long as your referred user continues to use the platform, you can continue earning commissions

- ✅ Earnings are automatically calculated based on the referred user’s actual spending

- ✅ Detailed earnings and accumulated commissions are available in your dashboard

---

## 3. How to Start Promoting?

##### Step 1: Register or Log In

Log in to your CafeScraper account. If you do not have an account, please register first.

##### Step 2: Get Your Exclusive Referral Link

Go to the Promoter Program page. The system will automatically generate your exclusive referral link or invitation code.

You can promote through:

- Social media platforms
- Blogs or websites
- Online communities
- Email recommendations
- Video tutorials

##### Step 3: Friends Register and Use the Platform

When your friends register through your link and generate valid spending, commissions will be automatically credited to your account.

---

## 4. Commission Settlement & Withdrawal

##### 1️⃣ Settlement

- Commissions are calculated in real time
- Withdrawals can be requested 30 days after meeting the minimum withdrawal amount (subject to current policy).

##### 2️⃣ Withdrawal Method

- PayPal withdrawals supported
- After submitting a withdrawal request, payment will be processed upon approval

##### 3️⃣ Minimum Withdrawal Amount

- For example: minimum withdrawal amount is **$20** (subject to current platform rules)

## 5. Promotion Rules

To ensure fairness, please note:

- ❌ Fake registrations or self-referral for arbitrage are strictly prohibited

- ❌ Manipulating commissions through fraudulent or abusive methods is prohibited

- ❌ Advertising content that violates laws or regulations is prohibited

If violations are detected, the platform reserves the right to cancel commissions and revoke promoter eligibility.

## 6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?**

No. You may invite an unlimited number of users.

**Q2: Can I refer myself?**

No. The system detects self-referral behavior.

**Q3: Can commissions be withdrawn?**

Yes. PayPal withdrawals are supported.

**Q4: Do commissions expire?**

Generally no, but specific terms are subject to current promotional policies.

**Q5: What happens if a referred user receives a refund?**

If the referred user’s order is refunded or invalidated, the corresponding commission may be deducted.
